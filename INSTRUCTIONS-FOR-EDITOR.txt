HOW TO EDIT THE XCHANGEBYTE PRESENTATION (ONLINE)
==================================================

Sufyan will send you a website link (e.g. https://something.vercel.app)

STEP 1 — OPEN THE LINK
-----------------------
Click the link Sufyan sends you. It opens in your browser.


STEP 2 — VIEW SLIDES
--------------------
- Click "Previous" or "Next" at the top
- OR use the left/right arrow keys on your keyboard


STEP 3 — EDIT THE TEXT
-----------------------
- Click on any text you want to change
- Type your changes
- Move to the next slide and repeat


STEP 4 — SAVE YOUR CHANGES
---------------------------
- Click the green "SAVE CHANGES" button at the top
- Wait for the "Saved!" message
- Your changes are now saved online


STEP 5 — SEE EACH OTHER'S CHANGES
----------------------------------
- If Sufyan made changes, click "REFRESH LATEST" to load them
- If you made changes, tell Sufyan to click "REFRESH LATEST" too


IMPORTANT
---------
- Always click SAVE CHANGES before closing the browser
- If you don't save, your edits will be lost
- You do NOT need to download or email any files
